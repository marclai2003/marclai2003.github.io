Research

My research interests are in discrete algorithms and optimization. I am particularly excited by theoretical problems that arise from practical settings, such as scheduling and network problems. My goal is to research efficient algorithms for discrete problems using techniques from combinatorial optimization, probability, and graph theory. Checkout my research projects and curated school work!
